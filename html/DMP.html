Dental and Mouth Problems

Description
  • Bad Breath (Halitosis) – Sign of dental disease or digestive issues.
  • Drooling or Difficulty Eating – Possible dental pain or oral infection.
  • Swollen Gums or Bleeding – Gingivitis or periodontal disease.
