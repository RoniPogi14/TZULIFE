Digestive and Stomach Issues

Description
  • Vomiting – Occasional vomiting might be normal, but frequent episodes need vet attention.
  • Diarrhea or Loose Stools – Could indicate food intolerance, infections, or parasites.
  • Constipation – Struggling to poop or infrequent bowel movements.
  • Bloated Stomach – Could be a sign of gas, digestive issues, or something more serious.
