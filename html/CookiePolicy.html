1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how TZULIFE ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They help improve website functionality, analyze site traffic, and personalize user experience.
Cookies may be session cookies (temporary, deleted after you close your browser) or persistent
cookies (remain stored until manually deleted or expired).

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:
  • Essential Cookies – Necessary for website functionality, such as security and login authentication.
  • Analytics Cookies – Help us understand how users interact with our website and improve our services.
  • Preference Cookies – Store user preferences such as language settings.
  • Marketing Cookies – Used to deliver relevant content and advertisements based on user interests.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.
Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), which place their own cookies
on your device. These third parties have their own privacy policies regarding data collection and usage.
